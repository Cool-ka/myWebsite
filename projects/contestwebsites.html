CONTEST WEBSITES

A series of websites made specifically for online contests, as part of marketing campaigns for movies and video games. Websites such as these are supposed to catch attention of the users and present all the relevant information (prizes, contest task) in a clear and concise way.

Depending on the exact project, these websites could have involved additional scripts (e.g. voting mechanisms), picture galleries, or comments sections. Contest websites were published within a structure of a larger site, GRYOnline.pl, which meant they have to feature navigation elements similar to the ones on parent website.

These kinds of projects involved not only creation of the website itself, but also other supporting graphic creatives, which were used in the corresponding marketing campaigns as well.
