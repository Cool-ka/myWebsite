YOUR NEW DIETARY HABITS

Your New Dietary habits is a project that involved creating a product page and expert blog. To ensure that website does its job as point of sale, it features a sales widget, email subscription function and SSL encryption.

Furthermore, the website is fully responsive (width measured in % and font size in em). Since the product is about healthy eating, the website was designed to build trust and features soothing green color.

Supporting blog is based on Wordpress CMS and was placed in a subdirectory to ensure that content boosts the whole domain together with product page in search results. The CMS was also equipped with additional plugins (Instagram, SEO by Yoast).
